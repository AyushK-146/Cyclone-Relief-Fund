ABOUT CYCLONE RELIEF

Due to the Geographical structure of the Indian peninsula, the east coast facing the Bay of Bengal is prone to cyclones. Cyclones are natural calamity which can leave a trail of damage. Roads and Railways get blocked, roofs of houses are blown over, trees collapse and lives of those living near coasts get affected. Recently severe cyclonic storms have ravaged the coasts of Odisha and West Bengal. Many fishermen have lost their livelihood, their homes and boats are destroyed. Sea water has damaged farmland and gale and thunder have created havoc.

Your donation can help the community come back to their normal lives. It can repair all the damage done and give the ones who suffered an opportunity to excel.

A contribution made in kind can help the affected rebuild homes and schools. This can in turn increase literacy. Your little contribution can bring smile to millions, grant them good health and sanitation post cyclone and make their livelihood sustain. This is our time to contribute as much as possible and be a reason for their well being.
